Who it is for

This is not for everyone. Here is who it is for.

You have already taken the assessment. You know which areas are low. You are not looking for someone to tell you what is wrong — you are looking for someone to help you actually fix it.

You are functional but not where you want to be. Nothing is catastrophically broken. But the gap between where you are and where you could be has been there long enough that you have stopped pretending it is going to close on its own.

You are done trying other people's prescriptions. You have read the books, followed the systems, copied the routines. They work for someone. They did not work in your system. You want something built for where you actually are.

You want to do the work, not talk about it. Coaching here is diagnostic and prescriptive — not supportive and exploratory. We figure out what is running, we build what replaces it, and you go do it. If you need someone to cheer you on, this is not the right room.
