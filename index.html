The Architecture

Eight areas. One life.

Every part of your life falls into one of these eight areas. They are connected. When one breaks down, it drags the others down with it. The diagnostic measures all eight — and finds the one that is pulling everything else backward.

Recovery
Sleep, stress regulation, active recovery, rest. The restoration time that determines what is available everywhere else.
Metabolic
Nutrition, hydration, supplementation, metabolic awareness. The fuel behind everything you do.
Physical
Strength, cardiovascular capacity, mobility, training. The physical foundation that carries everything else.
Cognitive
Focus, decision quality, learning, state management. How your mind processes demand and operates under pressure.
Temporal
Schedule, time protection, priorities, delegation. Whether your time belongs to you or to whoever asks.
Financial
Income, cash flow, stability, wealth building. The floor that determines whether your body feels safe.
Relational
Household, partnership, social world, conflict. The environment your body runs in every day.
Strategic
Direction, goals, execution, review, legacy. The area that decides what everything else is working toward.
