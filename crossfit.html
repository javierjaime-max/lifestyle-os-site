Our Story

FAMILY OWNED.
COMMUNITY DRIVEN.

We started CrossFit OTL in 2015 with a simple belief: fitness should be accessible, challenging, and built around people who actually care about each other. Ten years later, that belief hasn't changed. Every class, every coach, every member—it's personal here.
